FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sample delivery take?

Domestic samples ship within 2–3 business days and arrive within 5–7 days. International samples may take 10–14 business days.

Can I return samples?

Sample kits are non-refundable, but the cost is credited toward your full tile order — making samples essentially free when you purchase.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide with insured, white-glove delivery. Samples ship free within the US, and at reduced rates internationally.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Most collections have a minimum of 1 square meter. Custom orders may have higher minimums — contact us for details.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. Upgrade anytime — previous purchases are credited toward the new plan.
