Ordering & Installation

How many samples can I order?

Trade professionals can order up to 10 complimentary samples per project. Homeowners and individual clients may request up to 5 complimentary samples. Standard shipping rates apply.

What is the typical lead time for international orders?

In-stock collections typically ship within 3-5 business days. For custom orders or direct-from-quarry shipments, lead times generally range from 4 to 8 weeks depending on the region and artisan schedule.

Are the handmade tiles perfectly uniform?

No, and that is by design! Collections like our Moroccan Zellige feature intentional variations in color, thickness, and glaze. These "imperfections" are what give the installed tile its characteristic organic beauty and depth.
